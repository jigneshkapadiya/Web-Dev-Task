Course description

This is a dual honours course, which means you'll spend roughly half your time in the partner department, the School of Mathematics and Statistics. You'll get a good grounding in both subjects, plus the chance to study specific areas in more detail. The subjects are given equal weighting.

The way we teach the accounting and financial management side is the same, in principle, as the single honours course. You take a lot of the same modules as single honours students.

Because you're studying two subjects, you don't get to look at accounting and financial management or mathematics in as much detail as you would on the single honours degrees, but the quality of your education is the same.

The two subjects complement each other, which opens up even more career opportunities.

Sheffield University Management School

Only five Russell Group business schools have the internationally-recognised Triple Crown accreditation. Sheffield is one of them. We teach business management from a truly global perspective. Our staff and students come from all over the world. What we teach is based on pioneering research into the challenges facing businesses everywhere.

School of Mathematics and Statistics

At Sheffield, the oldest of the sciences is as relevant as ever. We teach modules on the financial, engineering and medical applications of maths. We also offer a degree combining maths with philosophy. For us, maths is exciting, fascinating, mind-expanding. And useful. Very, very useful.


What our graduates do

Graduates from all our courses go to work for companies like Barclays, KPMG, Proctor & Gamble, Rolls-Royce and Tesco, in accounting, customer relations, human resource management, information systems, marketing, and operations management and strategy.

Our graduates find work with multinationals and NGOs because they know how to deal with the international market and they understand the social and cultural aspects of doing business on a global scale.

Many of our graduates are successful entrepreneurs and consultants.
